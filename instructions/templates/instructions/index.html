Welcome to Wordwell




Wordwell is a fun and stimulating game for word game enthusiasts. Come join us and share your thoughts on our friendly message board - everyone is welcome!

How to play Wordwell


Connect the letters on the board vertically, horizontally and diagonally to create as many words as possible before the timer runs out!
Can you find them all? Use each tile just once per word.

Scoring is based on the length of words.
A word must be at least 3 letters to be valid.
Word length is scored as follows:

  • 3 letters - 1 point
  • 4 letters - 2 points
  • 5 letters - 3 points
  • 6 letters - 4 points
  • 7 letters - 5 points
  • 8+ letters - 6 points
